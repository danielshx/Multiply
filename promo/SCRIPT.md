## Shot list (for external AI video tools)

### Shot 1 · Cold open · 0.0–3.0s
> Extreme close-up macro shot of a single glowing white particle floating in pitch black, slowly expanding and pulsing with violet light bleeding outward. Anamorphic lens flare. 24fps, shallow depth of field, dust motes, cinematic, Denis Villeneuve style.

### Shot 2 · Problem · 3.0–7.0s
> Overhead shot of an empty desk at night: a ringing desk phone, a cold cup of coffee, a stack of paper, a dim laptop screen showing "Follow up? / Follow up? / Follow up?". Deep shadows, teal and orange color grade. Camera slowly pushes in. Moody, tired.

### Shot 3 · "Until now." · 7.0–10.0s
> The phone on the desk suddenly lights up violet from within. The entire room floods with purple light. Time freezes. A single dust mote hovers. Anamorphic flare. Blade Runner 2049 palette.

### Shot 4 · Title · 10.0–14.0s
> Black void. The word "multiply" materializes letter by letter in bright white sans-serif, each letter landing with a violet shockwave ring. Subtitle "THE SWARM OUTREACH ENGINE" fades in below in monospace. Very slight scanline texture. 8K, rendered type, crystal sharp.

### Shot 5 · Orchestra · 14.0–19.0s
> Abstract glowing neural-network diagram: seven luminous orbs arranged around a central pulsing core, each orb labeled with a role — Prospector, Researcher, Qualifier, Negotiator, Closer, Signal Hunter, Personaliser. Soft violet-cyan glow, orbiting animation, cosmic feel, low-poly futurist aesthetic.

### Shot 6 · Swarm · 19.0–24.0s
> Bird's-eye view of a dark control room with 25 floating holographic terminals in a 5×5 grid, each showing a live audio waveform and a status badge (COLD, WARM, HOT, BOOKED). Badges flicker color — teal, amber, pink, green — as the camera slowly pushes in. Bloomberg-terminal-meets-Minority-Report.

### Shot 7 · Graph · 24.0–27.0s
> A living knowledge graph — 94 luminous nodes connected by 157 edges, pulsing and reorganizing itself. Color-coded by type (persona, objection, outcome, region). The camera orbits slowly. Particles drift. Looks like a brain firing.

### Shot 8 · KPI · 27.0–29.0s
> Four giant kinetic numbers on a dark stage: "25 · 5 · 1 · 12", each labeled DIALS · CONNECTS · MEETINGS · LEARNINGS. Numbers count up rapidly, final digit lands with a violet flash. Typography cinematic, huge, Apple-keynote-meets-cyberpunk.

### Shot 9 · Close · 29.0–30.0s
> Final logo card: "multiply" in bright white huge sans-serif on pitch black, URL underneath in violet monospace. Subtle violet particle drift. Hold 1 second, cut to black.

## Music brief

- 60-90 BPM · Am or Dm · minor-key tension → major release at 7.0s "until now"
- Instrumentation: sub bass pulse, arpeggiated synth, sparse piano notes, light snare at 14s, swell to full at 19s, drop to minimal at 24s, big impact at 27s
- Reference tracks: Hans Zimmer *Dune* main theme texture; M83 *Outro*; Trent Reznor *The Social Network* opening
- Royalty-free search: "cinematic tech drone build" · "minimal dark ambient pulse" · "inspirational tech minimal"

## Production notes

- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 hero, but mirror to 1:1 and 9:16 for social
- **Color grade:** crushed blacks, slight teal in shadows, violet in highlights, desaturated midtones (`-15` saturation except on accent color `#7c5cff`)
- **Sound design:**
  - 0.0s — deep sub hum starts
  - 3.0s — single phone ring (filtered, distant)
  - 7.0s — hard cut to silence → reverb swell
  - 10.0s — title impact (sub boom + glass shatter + synth swell)
  - 19.0s — swarm whoosh (25 overlapping UI chirps panned L-R)
  - 24.0s — data-stream stutter (filtered hi-hats + morse-code-like chirps)
  - 27.0s — each KPI counts with an SMS-send blip
  - 29.5s — final logo impact (sub boom, reverb tail cut on last frame)
